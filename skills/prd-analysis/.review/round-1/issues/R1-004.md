# SKILL.md claims wrong checker counts (5/6 vs actual 8/16)

## Offending text (verbatim)

Line 13 of `SKILL.md`:

> "...iterative review/revise rounds driven by **5 script-type checkers** (CR-S-001..CR-S-005) and **6 LLM-type checkers** (CR-L-001..CR-L-006), with a summarizer and judge closing each round."

## Why this fails

`common/review-criteria.md` defines **8 script-type criteria** (CR-S01 through CR-S08, all confirmed present in R1-W-002's self-review which sampled all 24 entries) and **16 LLM-type criteria** (CR-L01 through CR-L16). The SKILL.md claim of "5 script-type and 6 LLM-type" understates both counts and contradicts the authoritative criteria registry.

This is a documentation-truth issue: a user reading SKILL.md to understand what the skill does will form a false expectation about review depth. It is also a cross-artifact consistency failure between two focus leaves (SKILL.md and review-criteria.md).

Replace "5 script-type checkers (CR-S-001..CR-S-005) and 6 LLM-type checkers (CR-L-001..CR-L-006)" with "8 script-type checkers (CR-S01..CR-S08) and 16 LLM-type checkers (CR-L01..CR-L16)".
